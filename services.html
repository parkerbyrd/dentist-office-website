Services

Only the best for our patients

We offer a wide range of services from check-ups to same-day appointment emergency treatment:

We offer:

Preventive Oral Hygiene Instruction:

This includes proper brushing, flossing and eating habits, ideal fluoride levels and the use of pit and fissure sealants.

Comprehensive Orthodontic Treatment:

We carefully monitor growth and development to ensure timely correction of tooth and jaw malpositions to reduce future needs for braces.

Comprehensive Restorative Dentistry:

This includes children's fillings, crowns and prosthesis, including treatment of the often devastating nursing bottle decay.

Emergency Treatment:

Emergencies will be promptly addressed.
